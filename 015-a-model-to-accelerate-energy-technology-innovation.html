Empty Waymo cars are converging on one Atlanta cul-de-sac. No one can explain why

A normally quiet Atlanta neighborhood has suddenly found itself flooded with traffic early in the mornings. It’s not tourists. It’s not new neighbors. In fact, it’s not people at all—but an overwhelming amount of driverless cars. The cars are from robotaxi company Waymo, which has been operating in Atlanta since June of 2025.
